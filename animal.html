How Does It Work?

This Animal Face Test uses advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning technology to analyze facial features. We utilize a custom model trained with Google's Teachable Machine, which has learned to distinguish between characteristics commonly found in dogs and cats.

When you upload an image, it is processed locally on your device using TensorFlow.js. This means your photos are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your privacy is protected. The model breaks down the image into patterns and compares them against its training data to provide a fun prediction of whether you have a "Dog Face" or a "Cat Face".

Give it a try with photos of yourself, your friends, or even your pets! Please note that this is for entertainment purposes only.
